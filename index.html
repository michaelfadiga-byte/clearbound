The problem

Carrier appetite changes constantly.
Broker workflows haven't.

Updates live in emails, PDFs, portals, and memory — making it impossible to know what underwriters will accept today.

⚠

Wasted submissions

Risks sent to carriers who quietly tightened appetite weeks ago. Time lost. Relationships strained.

⊘

Burned UW relationships

Repeated mismatched submissions erode carrier trust and reduce future responsiveness.

↻

Renewal surprises

Appetite shifts surface at renewal, not before — leaving brokers scrambling with no time to remarket.

◇

Tribal knowledge

Critical underwriting intel lives in one producer's head. When they leave, the knowledge walks out the door.

The solution

Underwriting intelligence,
not another system of record.

Clearbound sits above your AMS. We don't replace existing tools — we capture the underwriting signal that normally lives in inboxes and memory.

Δ

Guideline change detection

Automatically tracks carrier guide updates. Surfaces only what materially changed — new requirements, tightened thresholds, or relaxed controls. Plain-English action guidance.

⊕

Carrier appetite matching

Maps a cyber risk profile to a ranked carrier short-list based on stated guidelines and observed market behavior. Includes rationale and confidence scoring.

✓

Submission readiness scoring

Scores your submission 0-100 before it goes out. Flags missing controls (MFA, EDR, backups), unrealistic limits, and documentation gaps.

↻

Market-reality calibration

Learns from outcomes — declines, quotes, binds, and UW feedback — to distinguish written rules from how underwriting actually behaves in practice.
