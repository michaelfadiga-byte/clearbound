The problem

Carrier appetite changes constantly.
Broker workflows haven't.

Updates live in emails, PDFs, portals, and individual memory — making it impossible to know what underwriters will actually accept today.

01

Wasted submissions

Risks sent to carriers who quietly tightened appetite weeks ago. Producer time lost. Underwriter patience spent.

02

Burned UW relationships

Repeated mismatched submissions erode carrier trust and reduce responsiveness on future placements.

03

Renewal surprises

Appetite shifts surface at renewal, not before — leaving brokers scrambling with no time to remarket.

04

Tribal knowledge

Critical placement intelligence lives in one producer's head. When they leave, the knowledge walks out the door.

The solution

Underwriting intelligence,
not another system of record.

Clearbound sits above your AMS. We don't replace existing tools — we capture the signal that normally lives in inboxes and memory.

I

Guideline change detection

Automatically tracks carrier guide updates. Surfaces only what materially changed — new requirements, tightened thresholds, relaxed controls. Plain-English action guidance, delivered before the next submission.

II

Carrier appetite matching

Maps a cyber risk profile to a ranked carrier shortlist based on stated guidelines and observed market behaviour. Includes rationale and confidence scoring for every recommendation.

III

Submission readiness scoring

Scores your submission 0–100 before it goes out. Flags missing controls, unrealistic limits, and documentation gaps — so your producer knows exactly what to fix.

IV

Market-reality calibration

Learns from outcomes — declines, quotes, binds, and underwriter feedback — to distinguish written guidelines from how carriers actually behave in practice. This is the moat.
